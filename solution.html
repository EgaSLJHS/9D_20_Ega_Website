Buy And Protect Forest Land

One of the best ways to save Borneo is by helping us purchase various parts of the land, allowing it to be permanently protected. By owning land of Borneo's rainforest, we are able to rightfully prevent any plans for deforestation which stops:

  • Logging
  • Forest fires
  • Planting of palm oil trees

This helps protect more than just trees, with animals, plants, rivers, and soil being ensured to never be disrupted since no human can enter the lands. Purchasing land isn't just for corporations to destroy them, but it also is done by groups like us who try to keep one of the world's mostg important places protected.

03

Planting Trees

Donations also help plant the native trees that have been chopped down within deforested areas of the Borneo rainforest. Reforestation helps rebuild various ecosystems by providing the right trees that help other forms of life thrive. Soil is able to revover, animals have a place to live, and water won't run away into floods.

  • Dipterocarp trees: Make the forest's canopy which helps give shade and humidity.
  • Meranti trees: Hardwood trees that protect soil and form the forest
  • Fig trees: Provide a food source for animals like orangutans, allowing them to live
  • Ironwood: Durable trees that make up the forest
