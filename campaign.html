Guardians of Borneo Campaign

This campaign exists to turn concern into protection by connecting people, funding, and action around the future of the Borneo rainforest.

Mission & Vision

Our missions is simple yet important: to protect and restore Borneo's rainforest before it's too late. The lush forest is one of the most important ecosystems on Earth, which is why it needs to be preserved. By working together with us, we can preserve its nature, protect its wildlife, and ensure that the future will still be able to enjoy it's beauty while the flora and fauna thrive.

Why Borneo Matters

Borneo's rainforest is one of the richest ecosystems in the world, filled with unique wildlife like orangutans, elephants, and leopards. These forests regulate climate, produce clean air, and help protect nearby communities from flooding. Protecting Borneo means protecting life far beyond the forest itself.

The Problem

Right now, Borneo's rainforest is disappearing. Large areas are being cleared for palm oil plantations, illegal logging, and forest fires that burn away entire habitats. Every day, more wildlife loses its home while the planet sinks deeper into climate damage.
