The People Behind The Canopy

Who really are we? Meet the founding fathers of this campaign, the pioneers of Borneo Rainforests' safety.

Meet The Team

Below are the 3 creators of 'Guardians of Borneo'. Without them, no land would be bought, more trees would be chopped, and more animals will die. Each of them brang huge progress in stopping the dangerous deforestation.

Environmental Research Scientist

Meet Raphael Aldevaro Sumadi our leading environmental research scientist. He focuses on studying the ecosystem of Borneo's rainforest, collects data on deforestation, animal/plant loss, alongside the damage done to the climate. He helps us indentify what exactly is causing all of the problems we want to abolish while also providing scientific insight on what actions we should take.

Solution Strategist

This is Michael Ega Mahendra which is our solution stragetist and most of all, the campaign's founder. He brought together Aldevaro alongside Lathan with one idea, to preserve Earth's most vital ecosystem. With the help of those 2 men, Ega was able to design solutions like land protection, reforestation plans, and animal rehabilitation. Without him, every inch of Borneo's rainforest would be gone.

Campaign Director

The reason you are even reading this is because of Lathan Axel Aurelio. He leads promotion and finds funding efforts by searching for investors like you, donors, and even global partners like UNICEF. His role in this campaign is what makes our ideas possible, by securing and providing financial support needed to turn our dreams into reality.
